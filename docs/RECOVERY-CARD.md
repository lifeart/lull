# Recovery card (print and tape near the hub)

**Controller shows everything red / "hub unreachable":**
1. Power-cycle the hub box. Wait 60 seconds.
2. Reopen the Controller on your phone. It reconnects automatically.

**One room says "offline" or "needs tap":**
1. Walk to that device, tap the screen (wake it), tap "resume" if the screen is dark.
2. If it doesn't reconnect: re-open the Speaker page from the Home Screen icon and tap "Arm".

**Sound stopped but device looks connected:**
- Check the ring/silent switch and volume buttons on that device.
- Tap Stop then Start from the Controller.

**Nothing works at 3am:**
- Any phone with the noise file can be a stopgap. Fix the device properly in the morning
  and re-run the bedtime pre-flight before the next night.

Do the **bedtime pre-flight** ("Check all rooms") every night — it catches a dead device
while you're awake instead of at 3am.
